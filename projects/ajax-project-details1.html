Twitter Cards

webdesign / webdevelopment
General Info

Global Launch of Twitter Card Voting.

Project Details

We wanted to leverage the real-time broadcast nature of Twitter as a live voting platform. By allowing fans to vote (without tweeting/hashtagging) and share directly from their timeline, Twitter users experienced a seamless social media engagement experience.

Results

The first card vote for AMAs proved to be highly popular and significantly increased awareness and levels of social engagement leading up to the live event. Following up from the AMAs, a Twitter card vote was created for The Miss Universe competition. The campaign was very successful and trended worldwide. For Miss Universe, the Twitter card vote increased social engagement on Twitter by 8% and also led to a 10% increase in reach and impressions.
