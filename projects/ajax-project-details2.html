Project Details

Viggle Live

webdesign / webdevelopment
General Info

Viggle Live, the entertainment and rewards platform, chose Telescope's technology and social engagement solutions to create a richer play along experience for their users.

Project Details

We created a range of custom and templated play along games which are all powered through our Connect Live social marketing and digital platform. Viggle has integrated Telescope's technology and our platform supports numerous Viggle shows and activations every day. Using our intuitive CMS tools, the content and play along experiences are created, published and automated through Connect Live.

Results

The partnership has proven to be very successful with Telescope expanding their products and services for Viggle to drive increased engagement around their TV content.
